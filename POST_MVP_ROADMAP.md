# Decifra — Post-MVP Roadmap

> Everything valuable that is not required for launch. This is a product roadmap, organized by customer/business value — it intentionally excludes pure technical debt, cleanup, testing, and architecture work, which is tracked separately in `PROJECT_TASKS.md`. An item only appears here if it's something a customer or the business would notice and value. See `MVP_SPEC.md` for what ships first.

## AI

- **AI Advisor** — personalized guidance and recommendations based on a user's specific analyzed document.
- **Document Comparison** — compare two documents or two versions of the same document side by side.
- **Legal Research Assistant** — surface relevant context (laws, precedent, norms) related to a document's terms.
- **Document Chatbot** — ask free-form follow-up questions about an analyzed document.
- **Contract Simulation ("Reality Mode")** — model how a contract plays out under different real-world scenarios, as a single polished feature.
- **Expanded document-type coverage** — deeper, specialized compliance checks beyond pay slips (e.g., lease-specific or insurance-specific checks).

## Lawyer Platform

- **Lawyer ratings & reviews** from clients.
- **Lawyer selection / smart routing** — let clients choose a lawyer or route intelligently by specialty/availability.
- **Lawyer earnings dashboard** with payout tracking.
- **Expanded opinion template library**, per document type.
- **Turnaround-time commitments (SLA)** for lawyer responses, tracked and shown to clients.

## Collaboration

- **PDF export** of a completed analysis.
- **Shareable read-only links** — share an analysis with a landlord, employer, family member, or co-signer.
- **Activity history** — a user-facing log of what's happened with a document.
- **Shared notes/comments** that another party (e.g., a collaborator) can see.
- **Team/shared folders** for households or small groups.

## Enterprise

- **Multi-seat organization accounts.**
- **SSO login** for enterprise/business customers.
- **Bulk document upload.**
- **Custom/negotiated enterprise pricing and contracts.**
- **Admin/business dashboards** for enterprise account managers.
- **Dedicated account support** with its own SLA.

## Marketing

- **Referral program.**
- **SEO-optimized content and landing pages.**
- **Lifecycle/drip email campaigns.**
- **Affiliate/partner program** (e.g., with real-estate agencies, HR platforms).
- **In-app upsell prompts** driven by usage patterns.

## Analytics

- **User-facing usage dashboard** — documents analyzed over time, risks flagged, value delivered.
- **Internal business intelligence dashboard** — conversion funnels, upgrade trends, lawyer-case throughput.
- **Revenue/payment reconciliation reporting** for finance and operations.

## Integrations

- **E-signature integration** for reviewed documents.
- **Calendar integration** for scheduling lawyer reviews.
- **Accounting/invoicing software integration.**
- **Public API** for partners (real-estate platforms, HR software) to submit documents for analysis.
- **Webhook notifications** for enterprise customers' own systems.

## Mobile

- **Native iOS/Android apps.**
- **Mobile camera-first document capture flow.**
- **Push notifications.**

## Infrastructure

*(Customer- and business-facing infrastructure commitments only — internal engineering hygiene is tracked in `PROJECT_TASKS.md`, not here.)*

- **Public status page and uptime SLA** for paying customers.
- **Scaling analysis throughput** to keep turnaround time consistent as volume grows.
- **Compliance certifications** (e.g., SOC 2) to support enterprise sales.

## Future Ideas

*(Speculative — directional, not yet scoped or committed.)*

- AI-assisted negotiation guidance (suggested talking points/redlines based on a document's analysis).
- Financial-literacy / educational content hub.
- Community Q&A around common document types.
- Browser extension for scanning documents encountered while browsing.
- White-label platform for law firms or HR platforms to offer Decifra under their own brand.
